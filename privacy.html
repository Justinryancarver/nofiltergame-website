Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how No Filter Game ("we," "us," "our") handles information when you use our mobile application or website (collectively, the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information needed to operate the Service:

  • Player name: The name you enter when joining or creating a room. This is visible only to other players in your room and is deleted when the room ends.
  • Anonymous user identifier: When you use the app, we generate an anonymous ID via Firebase Anonymous Authentication. This ID is not linked to your real identity, email, or phone number.
  • Game data: The cards you play, scores, and room state during active games. This data is automatically deleted within 24 hours of a game ending.
  • Crash and error reports: If the app crashes, we may receive automated reports through Sentry to help us fix bugs. These reports do not include your name or game content.

We do not collect: your email address, phone number, real name, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Operate the multiplayer game functionality
  • Display your name to other players in your room
  • Identify and fix bugs or crashes
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase: Hosts the real-time game database and provides anonymous authentication. Firebase's privacy policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Sentry: Receives anonymized crash reports. Sentry's privacy policy: sentry.io/privacy

Data Retention

Active game rooms are stored only while the game is in progress and for a brief period afterward. Rooms older than 24 hours are automatically deleted. Anonymous authentication tokens persist on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Children's Privacy

No Filter is rated for users 17 and older and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the Service, please contact us at support@nofiltergame.com and we will delete any associated data.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect personal information tied to your identity, there is little personal data to access, modify, or delete. To remove all locally stored data, simply uninstall the app or clear its data through your device settings. To request deletion of any specific information, contact us at support@nofiltergame.com.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Game data is protected by Firebase Security Rules that prevent unauthorized access. While we take reasonable measures to protect data, no system is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or on this page. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.

Contact Us

Questions or concerns about privacy? Email us at support@nofiltergame.com.
